# Credit Card and Prepaid Card Disputes

Credit card complaints often involve billing errors, merchant disputes, unauthorized charges, rewards issues, interest charges, or trouble resolving a purchase dispute.

Support should collect the transaction date, merchant name, amount, dispute reason, prior contact attempts, and any supporting documentation. Explain whether the case is a billing dispute, fraud claim, chargeback review, or servicing issue. Escalate when the customer reports repeated unauthorized activity or a missed dispute deadline.
